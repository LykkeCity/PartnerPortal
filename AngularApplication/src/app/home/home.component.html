Lykke

Lykke coins are colored coins registered on the Bitcoin blockchain, representing shares of Lykke Corp. Each registered share of Lykke Corp corresponds to 100 Lykke coins.

How it works?

Become our partner and we will help you build something awesome
  • 1. Become our partner

    Get in touch with us and tell us more about your idea, what you want to implement and we will consult you on the best way to do it.

  • 2. Solve Compliance

    We can provide legal assistance and help you be compliant. Each asset that will be listed as a token needs to be compliant with a jurisdiction in a particular country.

  • 3. Settlement and smart contracts

    We support multiple settlement methods including Trusted settlement used for high-frequency operations and Blockchain settlement (ERC20 and Colored Coins).

  • 4. Launch in the Ecosystem

    Once the token delivery is planned and it’s time to launch it in the ecosystem using our ICO Platform. We have created the infrastructure for secure crowdfunding that you can leverage.
